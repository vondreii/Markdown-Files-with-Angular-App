# Delicious Strawberry Recipe
___

This is how you make an awesome Strawberry Cheesecake:

## Ingredients

#### For the base:
* Biscuits 
* Butter 
 
#### For the cheesecake:
* Some sort of cream
* Cream Cheese
* Something else
* Strawberry syrup?

## Steps
* Crush the biscuits
* Mix biscuits into the butter 
* Let it set in the fridge for 10 minutes
* Mix the cream with the strawberry syrup
* Put it ontop of the base
* Put it in the fridge for 10 hours
